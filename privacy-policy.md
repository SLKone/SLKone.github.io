## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 Rights Under GDPR (EEA, UK, and Switzerland Residents)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable national laws:

- **Right of access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"):** Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
- **Right to restriction of processing:** Request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
- **Right to data portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Right to object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.
- **Right to withdraw consent:** Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.
- **Right to lodge a complaint:** File a complaint with your local supervisory authority (e.g., ICO in the UK, or the relevant EU data protection authority).

Our lawful bases for processing personal data are set out in Section 4. Where we rely on legitimate interests, you may request details of our balancing assessment.

### 6.2 Rights Under CCPA / CPRA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

- **Right to know:** Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the business purpose, and the third parties with whom it is shared.
- **Right to delete:** Request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
- **Right to correct:** Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
- **Right to opt out of sale or sharing:** We do **not** sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
- **Right to limit use of sensitive personal information:** We do not use sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to provide our Services.
- **Right to non-discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

**Categories of personal information collected** (within the past 12 months):

- Identifiers (name, email, IP address)
- Commercial information (engagement and project records)
- Internet or network activity (usage data, cookies)
- Professional or employment-related information
- Inferences drawn from the above

**Authorized Agent:** California residents may designate an authorized agent to make requests on their behalf. We may require verification of the agent's authority.

To exercise any of the rights described above, contact us at:

We will respond to verified requests within **30 days** (GDPR) or **45 days** (CCPA), with one permissible extension of an additional 30–45 days where reasonably necessary. We will verify your identity before processing requests to protect your data from unauthorized disclosure or deletion.

## 7. Email Communications and CAN-SPAM Compliance

SLKone sends commercial email communications in compliance with the **CAN-SPAM Act** and, where applicable, **CASL** and **GDPR consent requirements**.

- **Identification:** All commercial emails clearly identify SLKone LLC as the sender.
- **Subject lines:** We do not use deceptive or misleading subject lines.
- **Physical address:** All commercial emails include our valid physical mailing address.
- **Opt-out mechanism:** Every commercial email includes a clear and conspicuous unsubscribe link or instructions.
- **Opt-out processing:** Unsubscribe requests are honored within **10 business days**.
- **No deceptive routing:** We do not use false or misleading header information.

To unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time:
- Click the **Unsubscribe** link in any marketing email, or
- Email **contact@slkone.com** with the subject line "Unsubscribe"

Transactional and account-related emails (e.g., password resets, security alerts, platform notifications) are not subject to opt-out and will continue as necessary to maintain your account.
